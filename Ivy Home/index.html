AAA Speckle Park is owned by Denis and Theresa Roberts, and the property is situated just outside Oberon, New South Wales.

We believe the breed’s high carcass yield, superior meat quality and excellent taste sets it apart from other breeds and provides the opportunity for producers to attain premium prices for their cattle. The breed’s ability to finish on grass and offer consistently high carcass yields is particularly beneficial in a market where consumers are increasingly attracted to naturally finished beef.

Denis and Theresa have sourced some of the leading genetics in Australia and from Canada to develop a stud based on elite genetics of the breed. We are undertaking recipient programs, which enables us to produce premium stud and commercial cattle for sale to other breeders throughout Australia

AAA Speckle Park is owned by Denis and Theresa Roberts, and the property is situated just outside Oberon, New South Wales.

Denis and Theresa purchased the property at Oberon and wanted a cattle breed that could add true value to the beef Industry. Careful research into the beef market and discussions with Dale Humphries (from Wattle Grove Speckle Park), lead to the decision to invest in Speckle Park cattle.
